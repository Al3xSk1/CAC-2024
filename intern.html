Internships

Internships are a great way to show experience in anything. It is very hard to find internships targeted toward high-school students especially if you are not a senior. In my high-school, my teacher told me "it is such a shame that people have to pay for internships, think of the inequity."

I have completed 2 internships while in high-school. Both of which from a small company, which thanks to them, I was able to make some income fully remote. Shoutout to the Work-Based Learning Alliance.

Work-Based Learning Alliance website

If your school has an IT department, ask them if they would be interested in summer internships or after-school internships for volunteering or even pay!
